## Weaker Or Partially Verified Signals

- platform-internal rankings, user scores, and "annual number-one" claims that only appear on mirrors, retailer pages, or user reviews
- imported-title ranking positions that may drift over time
- secondary commentary that repeats platform claims without independent confirmation

These may inform intuition but should not become hard benchmark rules by themselves.

## Distillation Use

Use the sources above to extract:

- historical fiction: institution, detail, and power-chain logic
- realism: ordinary life under public or structural pressure
- suspense/mystery: fair information control and payoff order
- profession-growth romance: career legitimacy plus relationship warmth under constraint
- ensemble game fiction: rules clarity, group differentiation, and line interference
- literary/serious fiction: formal necessity, residue, and depth landing in scene

Never turn a single title into a mandatory template.
