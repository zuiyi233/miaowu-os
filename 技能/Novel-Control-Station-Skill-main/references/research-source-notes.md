# Research Source Notes

This file records the public high-signal sources used to distill the foundational canon for `novel-control-station`.

It is a curated research backbone, not a claim of literal coverage of every published novelist, critic, or prize winner on earth.

## Professional Writers And Craft

- George Orwell, "Why I Write"
  - https://www.orwellfoundation.com/the-orwell-foundation/orwell/essays-and-other-works/Why%20i%20write/
  - Writing motive should serve truth, politics, aesthetics, and historical witness rather than empty performance.
- Haruki Murakami, The Paris Review, "The Art of Fiction No. 182"
  - https://www.theparisreview.org/interviews/2/the-art-of-fiction-no-182-haruki-murakami
  - Keep the surface easy to enter even when the structure carries complexity below.
- John Irving, The Paris Review, "The Art of Fiction No. 200"
  - https://www.theparisreview.org/interviews/4836/the-art-of-fiction-no-200-john-irving
  - Long fiction benefits from known ending pressure and structural intent.
- Ernest Hemingway, The Paris Review, "The Art of Fiction No. 21"
  - https://www.theparisreview.org/interviews/4825/the-art-of-fiction-no-21-ernest-hemingway
  - Sentence truth, revision, and exactness matter more than rhetorical display.

## Criticism And Narrative Theory

- Henry James, Britannica summary of "The Art of Fiction"
  - https://www.britannica.com/topic/The-Art-of-Fiction-essay-by-James
  - Fiction must give the impression of life rather than obey narrow formulas.
- E. M. Forster, Britannica summary of "Aspects of the Novel"
  - https://www.britannica.com/biography/E-M-Forster
  - Plot is causality, not mere sequence; characters need depth beyond flat utility.
- Britannica, "Point of view"
  - https://www.britannica.com/art/point-of-view-literature
  - Point of view is an artistic and informational constraint, not a neutral container.
- Britannica, "Mikhail Bakhtin"
  - https://www.britannica.com/biography/Mikhail-Bakhtin
  - Novel strength comes from dialogic pressure and the coexistence of distinct voices.

## Nobel Literature Lectures

- Toni Morrison, Nobel Lecture
  - https://www.nobelprize.org/prizes/literature/1993/morrison/lecture/
  - Language can oppress or liberate; literary seriousness includes responsibility to living speech.
- Gao Xingjian, Nobel Lecture
  - https://www.nobelprize.org/prizes/literature/2000/gao/lecture/
  - Literature should bear witness to individual experience beyond doctrine and market coercion.
- Orhan Pamuk, Nobel Lecture
  - https://www.nobelprize.org/prizes/literature/2006/pamuk/lecture/
  - The novelist searches the hidden wounds of human life through patient inward attention.
- Mario Vargas Llosa, Nobel Lecture
  - https://www.nobelprize.org/prizes/literature/2010/vargas_llosa/lecture/
  - Fiction's lies can reveal truth about freedom, power, and dissatisfaction with reality.
- Kazuo Ishiguro, Nobel Lecture
  - https://www.nobelprize.org/prizes/literature/2017/ishiguro/lecture/
  - Fiction should build bridges across separations and widen moral imagination without simplification.

## Chinese Realism, Mao Dun Prize, And Epoch Resonance

- Chinese Writers Association / China Writers Network on Chen Zhongshi and rural China in "White Deer Plain"
  - https://www.chinawriter.com.cn/n1/2019/1223/c404064-31517522.html
  - Local society, clan order, and changing historical force can generate epic narrative pressure.
- China Writers Network on Liu Jianwei's "Era Trilogy"
  - https://www.chinawriter.com.cn/n1/2020/0916/c429370-31862956.html
  - Large-scale fiction should connect national change to ordinary people's lived routes.
- Chinese Social Sciences Net on the Mao Dun Prize's narrative evolution
  - https://www.cssn.cn/zx/bwyc/202311/t20231108_5696863.shtml
  - Chinese long fiction evolves by balancing grand history, local rootedness, and individual complexity.

## Distillation Rule

When these sources disagree in tone or method, keep only the common denominator that can be enforced in writing:

- human truth over formula
- causality over event pile
- living language over dead abstraction
- form justified by truth it reveals
- individual fate linked to social, historical, or institutional force
